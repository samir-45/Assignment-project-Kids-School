Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

At the Kinder stage (ages 3-6), children embark on a journey of exploration, creativity, and foundational learning.

Elementary School

Elementary school is where the foundation for lifelong learning is built. At this stage, children develop essential academic skills.

Middle (10-16) Years

Middle school is a turning phase where students grow socially, and emotionally. This stage bridges childhood and adolescence.

Our curriculum is designed to challenge and engage students at every level. We offer a wide range of subjects, including English, mathematics, science, social studies, and the arts. We also provide enrichment programs in areas such as technology, foreign languages, and sports to ensure a well-rounded education.
