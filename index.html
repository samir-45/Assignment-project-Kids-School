Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

At the Kinder stage (ages 3-6), children embark on a journey of exploration, creativity, and foundational learning.

Elementary School

Elementary school is where the foundation for lifelong learning is built. At this stage, children develop essential academic skills.

Middle (10-16) Years

Middle school is a turning phase where students grow socially, and emotionally. This stage bridges childhood and adolescence.
